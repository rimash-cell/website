05 Dark & Quiet Near-black frames with a single warm point of light. Used sparingly — one in a sequence, not as a theme. Drama through restraint.

Dark · Warm Point

One light source from within

Dark · Depth

Corridor receding into near-black

Use Rule

One dark image per six

In any sequence of images — website, press kit, SM — no more than 1 in 6 should be dark dominant. Otherwise the brand reads as gloomy, not considered.

The dark image works because the others are light. It's the pause, not the tone.

✓ One warm light source minimum ✗ Cold or blue-tinted darks

Person as anchor, space as story
