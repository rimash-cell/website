04 — Graphic Principles

01

Space is designed

Generous margins. Silence is as intentional as content. Never fill the frame.

02

One accent per layout

Bronze used once — not scattered. When it appears, it carries meaning. When it doesn't, everything else does.

03

Type as architecture

Weight, spacing, and silence are the design. Black 900 against ExtraLight 200 is the brand's visual signature.

04

Hairline rules only

Dividers at 0.5–1px. Nothing structural should shout. The content is the authority, not its container.

05

Gradient: atmosphere only

Max 7–13% opacity glow in hero. Atmosphere, not decoration. The field itself should feel calm.

06

No drop shadows

Everything sits on the surface. Nothing floats. The brand references the physical world — weight, material, presence.
