Chapter 03

The Lens

Photography is our primary medium for atmospheric proof. It must feel warm, desaturated, and human.

Lighting Directional, soft, natural. High-contrast "calm" shadows.
Grading Warm, desaturated. Reference: Linen, Stone, Raw Plaster. No cold blues.
Focus Depth of field focused on "Ritual Moments" (materials, hands, thresholds).
Avoid Wide-angle real estate shots, oversaturation, clinical artificial light.
