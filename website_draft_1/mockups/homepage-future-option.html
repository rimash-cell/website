About / reframed

Less corporate polish. More digital presence.

The current homepage already feels refined. This option pushes it toward a sharper, more futuristic atmosphere with interface cues, luminous framing, layered depth, and a more deliberate sense of motion and precision.

Monospaced labels and framed modules to create a digital rhythm

Direction summary

Futuristic does not need to become cold.

The strongest route is probably not a full redesign. It is keeping your current elegance, then adding a more digital atmosphere through color, framing, motion, and sharper typographic hierarchy.
