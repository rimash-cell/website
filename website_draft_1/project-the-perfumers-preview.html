Campaign

The Perfumers

The Perfumers invited fragrance lovers to become creators, blending and naming a scent shaped by their own memories, emotions, and identity.

UAE 2024 Khaltat

The creation of your story scent.

The Ask

Create a high-engagement campaign for Khaltat that goes beyond traditional perfume advertising by directly involving the audience in the act of creation. The objective was to build buzz across the UAE, generate large-scale participation and UGC, and translate the brand's meaning of 'mixing' into an interactive campaign mechanic that felt emotional, creative, and culturally resonant.

The Response

A 360 contest campaign built around a guided Mixing Kit, supported by campaign videos, social rollout, retail VM, installations, and a landing page—creating a participatory brand world across physical and digital touchpoints.

What I Led
  • Campaign Strategy & Mechanism
  • Creative Direction
  • Kit & Packaging Design
  • Physical Activation & VM
The Concept
The Perfumers

Rooted in Khaltat's meaning of mixing, the campaign turned perfume into a personal act of storytelling.

600+ participants, strong UGC, and virality on TikTok and Instagram across the UAE.
