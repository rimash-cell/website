The Ask

A brand entering a trophy-retail market needed a pop-up that felt permanent and halted intense mall footfall.

The Response

I replaced materials logic with atmosphere logic. Rather than competing visually in a loud mall, I built a quiet, olfactory threshold that paused shoppers instantly.

The Concept
The Ritual of Unveiling

The pop-up was conceived as a progression. Entry through raw material. Transition through atmosphere. Arrival at the signature blend.

Over 400+ unique blends created within a three-day window, leading to an immediate invitation to replicate the experience in two additional GCC markets.
